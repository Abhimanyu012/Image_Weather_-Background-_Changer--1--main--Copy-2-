Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI change the weather in the background?

Our AI uses advanced image segmentation to identify the background of your photo. Then, based on your prompt (e.g., "add snow"), it generates realistic weather elements like snowflakes, rain, fog, or sunlight and seamlessly blends them into the background, adjusting lighting and atmosphere for a natural look.

Is the Image Weather Background Changer free to use?

Yes, our basic Image Weather Background Changer tool is completely free to use with no registration required for a limited number of transformations. We may offer premium features or higher usage limits for registered users or paid plans in the future.

What kind of images work best with this tool?

Images with a clear distinction between the foreground subject and the background tend to yield the best results. Outdoor scenes, landscapes, and portraits with visible sky or expansive backgrounds are ideal. However, our AI is designed to work with a wide variety of images.

Can I adjust the intensity of the weather effects?

Currently, you can guide the intensity through your text prompt (e.g., "light rain" vs "heavy storm"). We are working on adding more direct controls for fine-tuning the intensity and specific parameters of the weather effects in future updates.
